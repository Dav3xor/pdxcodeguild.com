Premier $10,000

Limited to three quarterly, Premier Sponsors get the most access to students and get primary input on curriculum. In addition to involvement with the community on a regular basis and input on curriculum for three months, Premier Sponsors get to place their banner and job postings in the learning center, get their logo posted prominently on the website with a link to their page, get thanked publicly on Twitter and Facebook, get to pass out materials in new student packets and at events, and receive invites to monthly sponsor events.

Champion $5,000

Limited to four quarterly, Champion Sponsors also get access to students and input on curriculum. In addition to involvement with the community on a regular basis for three months, Champion Sponsors get to place their banner and job postings in the learning center, get their logo posted prominently on the website with a link to your page, get thanked publicly on Twitter and Facebook, get to pass out materials in new student packets and at events, and receive invites to monthly sponsor events.
