Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the program run?

As long as you need. For many people expect 4-6 months. The program is a continuous rolling cohort of people from different backgrounds, who are seeking different goals. For this reason our program is designed to support your learning wherever you are currently at. If you only want a taste of what development is like, a month may be enough. For most people they want to learn enough to become solid junior developers, and turn that into a career. In practice we expect most beginners (who vary in experience) to take around 4-6 months to reach their goal of getting a job programming. Every situation is unique though, so please, contact us so we can talk about your unique circumstances.

When is it? What days and times?

We promote flexibility with a block schedule. It can be weekdays, nights and weekends, or a combination that works for you. You can see our block schedule on our Value and Pricing page.

What does it cost?

See our Value and Pricing page for details. Our rates are monthly.

What languages are you teaching?

We're teaching what you want to know. Our instructors have a broad background including most common languages, but we can help you learn any language that you feel is important. Some common choices are the web front end with HTML, CSS, and Javascript (with jQuery), a web back end like Ruby on Rails, or Python with Django, lower level languages like C and C++, and business languages like C# and Java. We have expertise in all of the above. You should contact us to discuss what possibilities are right for you.
