Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the Jr Developer Class run, and what is the schedule?

The Junior developer class is twelve weeks of instruction, with a two week break. Class dates are Tuesday - Saturday November 4th - December 20 and January 6 - February 14. Core class times are Tuesdays - Fridays 1pm - 8pm and Saturdays 10am - 1pm. If you can't make all the core class times, alternate arrangements can be made.

What can I expect at Jr Developer Class, and how will that help me become a good jr developer?

We cover core python, and important developer practices like source control, testing, and debugging. Pair programming and group work, as well as personal portfolio projects help round you out as a developer. We'll also help prepare you to get a job, with resume and interviewing coursework as well.

How much does the Jr Developer Class cost? Can I make payments?

Tuition for the Junior developer class is $2850. Tuition can be broken up into three payments of $950 each. We offer a 5% discount for tuition paid in full before before the first day of class.

How do I apply?

Everyone who applies is unique, and we feel a face-to-face conversation is the best way we can get to know each other. To set an appointment, either contact us directly or use or use the linked form to schedule a chat

How long does the individualized career development program run?

As long as you need. For many people expect 4-6 months. The program is a continuous rolling cohort of people from different backgrounds, who are seeking different goals. For this reason our program is designed to support your learning wherever you are currently at. If you only want a taste of what development is like, a month may be enough. For most people they want to learn enough to become solid junior developers, and turn that into a career. In practice we expect most beginners (who vary in experience) to take around 4-6 months to reach their goal of getting a job programming. Every situation is unique though, so please, contact us so we can talk about your unique circumstances.

When is the individualized career development instruction? What days and times?

You create your own schedule. We organize the week into blocks, and you choose the blocks that work for you. It can be weekdays, nights and weekend, or any combination that fits your life. You can see our block schedule on our Value and Pricing page.

When does it start?

We enroll new students each month and run a rolling cohort where new students join and other students graduate on their own schedule. Because the instruction is individualized, new students start as soon as they are ready.

What does it cost?

We have flexible plans that accommodate your needs. Our rates our monthly. See our Value and Pricing page for details.

What languages are you teaching?

We're teaching what you want to know. Our instructors have a broad background including most common languages, but we can help you learn any language that you feel is important. Some common choices are the web front end with HTML, CSS, and Javascript (with jQuery), a web back end like Ruby on Rails, or Python with Django, lower level languages like C and C++, and business languages like C# and Java. We have expertise in all of the above. You should contact us to discuss what possibilities are right for you.
