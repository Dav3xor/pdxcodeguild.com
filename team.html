Colin Dabritz

Head Instructor

  • Email: Colin@PdxCodeGuild.com
  • Twitter: @ColinDabritz
  • Linked-in: Colin Dabritz
  • Git Hub: Colin Dabritz

Colin has been a paid software developer for 8 years, but likes to think he's been an engineer his whole life. After growing up in the Portland area and attending MHCC, PCC and OIT Portland, He graduated from Oregon State University in Corvallis with a double degree in Computer Science and Education.

He has instructed the lab portion of a computer hardware course, helped many peers in their educational journeys, and most recently has been a staff developer at the Business Solutions Group providing mentoring and hands on real world software development experience for students at OSU, as well as managing and architecting large software projects.

In February of 2013 he moved back to Portland to pursue his interests in technology and education. He volunteered at Portland Code School for the winter 2013 batch. He believes the future of education is happening now, and want to help build it. His latest adventure is PDX Code Guild.

Sheri Dover

Director

  • Email: Sheri@PdxCodeGuild.com
  • Twitter: @SheriDover
  • Linked-in: Sheri Dover

Sheri Dover is an instigator and connector for the creative class. She has a strong passion for community and a desire to empower others. This has led her to volunteer as a cheerleader in micro enterprise and startup communities.

Some of the activities Sheri has been involved with include founding the Corvallis Artisans Market to create a very low barrier to entry incubator, founding Intersector Workspace, a coworking space designed to encourage business collaborations, helped organize Corvallis Social Media Brigade, a grass roots small business support network, and volunteered for Willammette Innovators Network and Startup Weekend.
